As of **February 20, 2026 (UTC)**, the legacy tenant management routes have been removed:

- `/api/mcp/tenants`
- `/api/sdk/v1/management/tenants`

Use this canonical endpoint instead:

- `/api/sdk/v1/management/tenant-overrides`

## Removal Summary

| Milestone | Date (UTC) | Outcome |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Announce | February 16, 2026 | Legacy routes marked deprecated and wrapped to canonical behavior. |
| Remove | February 20, 2026 | Legacy wrappers removed after confirming zero legacy traffic. |
